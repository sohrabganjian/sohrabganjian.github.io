description: "The Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA) is a variational gate-based method for combinatorial optimization on quantum gate-based hardware. This tutorial derives QAOA from adiabatic evolution, explains the cost and mixer Hamiltonians, and maps a QUBO to a cost Hamiltonian. It then applies QAOA to a number partitioning instance, building the circuit in PennyLane, optimizing parameters with a classical optimizer, and interpreting the output distribution against uniform and near-optimal baselines."
